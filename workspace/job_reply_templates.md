## Quick Decision Matrix

| Criteria | Response |
|----------|----------|
| ✅ Software/AI/DX + Remote | → INTERESTED (enthusiastic) |
| ✅ Software/AI/DX + Remote unclear | → INTERESTED (ask about remote) |
| ✅ Software/AI/DX + NOT remote | → DECLINE (not remote) |
| ❌ Sales/Admin/Non-tech | → DECLINE (polite, brief) |
| ❌ Generic recruiter spam | → DECLINE or IGNORE |

---

## Usage Notes

1. **Customize company/role names** - Replace {company} and {position} placeholders
2. **Pick 2-3 relevant achievements** - Don't list everything, just what matches the role
3. **Match their language** - Japanese recruiters → Japanese reply, English → English
4. **Keep it concise** - Japanese: under 200 chars ideal, English: under 200 words
5. **Always include contact info** - Email + phone in signature
