# Heartbeat - Daily Job Email Scan

## Task: Daily Job Email Monitoring

**Schedule:** Check 2-3 times per day (morning 9-10, afternoon 14-15, evening 18-19)

**What to do:**
1. Run Gmail search: `after:yesterday (recruit OR 採用 OR 面接 OR interview OR 選考 OR job OR forkwell OR timee OR タイミー OR 求人)`
2. Identify job-related emails requiring action
3. Draft/send appropriate responses automatically
4. Report summary to user

**Auto-respond to:**
- Final interview invitations (schedule + confirm interest)
- Direct recruiter messages (express interest if remote/tech, polite decline if not matching)
- Interview scheduling requests (book via link or request times)

**Info-only (no response):**
- Thank you emails with "we'll search for matches"
- Job listing notifications
- Application confirmations

**Silent hours:** 23:00-08:00 (no notifications unless urgent interview within 24h)
